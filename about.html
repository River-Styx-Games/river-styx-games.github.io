Penguin
What's there to say? My name is 'Mike,' and I'm a four-time novelist, full-time amateur, and occasional screenwriter. Rumors of my employment by various three-letter agencies have been greatly exaggerated, though I've been known to cavort with cohorts in the five-O. Described as a madlad - or a mad genius, depending on who you ask - I write only after midnight, using a 1940s manual typewriter and typing with the muzzles of handguns duct-taped to me. (You may use your imagination figuring out how that works.) Setting aside my professional exploits, I'm a Dicecord OG, and you'll never convince me American Saikoro isn't Dice's masterpiece. Despite my many trips down the River Styx, I remain a bondservant of Christ and am blessed to be working with the very talented members of our dev team.

Dragon
Glasses are really versatile. First, you can have glasses-wearing girls take them off and suddenly become beautiful, or have girls wearing glasses flashing those cute grins, or have girls stealing the protagonist's glasses and putting them on like, "Haha, got your glasses!' That's just way too cute! Also, boys with glasses! I really like when their glasses have that suspicious looking gleam, and it's amazing how it can look really cool or just be a joke. I really like how it can fulfill all those abstract needs. Being able to switch up the styles and colors of glasses based on your mood is a lot of fun too! It's actually so much fun! You have those half rim glasses, or the thick frame glasses, everything! It's like you're enjoying all these kinds of glasses at a buffet. I really want Luna to try some on or Marine to try some on to replace her eyepatch. We really need glasses to become a thing in hololive and start selling them for HoloComi. Don't. You. Think. We. Really. Need. To. Officially. Give. Everyone. Glasses?

Mepphington
Yo yo, my name's Mepphy Mepphington, Lead Artist for this sick project and coolest Demon-Seagull fella around! I've done art professionally to semi-professionally for at least 10 years now, and I'm providing the Sprite art and Character design for this VN. With over a month of consecutive listening-to of Karen's discography in the last year, I'm in her top 0,005% of listeners, which basically translates to: "I'm a mega fan of alt. rap á la dice." I've not been a fan of her stuff for too long, but from the moment I first listened to Hazy Skyscraper, I knew she'd be an inspiration to me for months and years to come. Be it her art, her music, or her style, eventually everything came to inspire the way I live my day-to-day life, so it's an honor to work on this project with other talented members of the Dicecord.
